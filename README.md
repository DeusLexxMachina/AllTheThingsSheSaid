# All The Things She Said

A data walk-through of Administrator Arielle Roth's testimony at the June 30, 2026 House Energy & Commerce Subcommittee oversight hearing on NTIA broadband policy — answer-length analysis, topic breakdowns, and a contradiction timeline, presented as a scrollable single-page site.
